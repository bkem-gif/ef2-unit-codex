# Endless Frontier (EF1) — Update Timeline

Historical reference for **Endless Frontier – Idle RPG** (`com.ekkorr.endlessfrontier.global`), the
original game by **Ekkorr** that **EF2** succeeds. Pieced together from scattered sources — Ekkorr
never published a single consolidated changelog.

> **Sourcing & date confidence.** Reconstructed from four source tiers:
> - 🟢 **Firm (single day)** — the developer's official Facebook posts (`facebook.com/endlessfrontierGlobal/posts/<id>/`, which render the publish date verbatim) and the Apple App Store "What's New" `<time>` element.
> - 🟢 **Firm (archived store)** — Wayback Machine snapshots of the Google Play / App Store pages (the "Updated" date + "What's New" text as shown on that archived day).
> - 🟡 **±1–2 days** — apk4fun APK upload dates (consistently within a day of the FB announcement).
> - 🔴 **Bracketed** — versions with no indexed post, dated only by their neighbours.
>
> Store "Updated" dates and the dev's FB announcement date differ by a few days (Google/Apple
> processing lag); where both exist I use the FB announcement date. Launch is **Feb 2016**.

---

## The shape of it (read first)

- **Two recurring content pillars.** **Honor Units** — a numbered set of 4 units (one per tribe:
  Human/Elf/Undead/Orc) + a Couple Pet, shipped every few months; the best progress yardstick
  (13th in Dec 2018 → **26th in May 2024**). And **Guild Raid** — themed dungeons (Ancient Frozen
  Civilization, Swamp of Death, Fortress of Hell Lord, Burning Earth, Ancient Ruins) that gain new
  3-stage batches and "Extreme/Hell" raid pets over the years.
- **Stage growth:** the "Scenario" counter climbed 80,000 (Nov 2019) → 90,000 (Mar 2020) → 94,000
  (Apr 2020).
- **Newer systems layered on:** Unit Equipment (May 2020), 8-star Artifact 3-Transcendence (Dec
  2020), Airship fleet expansions (2020–2024), **Hall of Heroes** (Jan 2024).
- **The maintenance pivot:** after **v4.1.0 (Nov 2024)**, content stops — all of 2025 is "stability
  improvements." This lines up with Ekkorr shifting to **EF2**; EF1 is now in maintenance.

## Known gaps
- **v1.6–2.2 (~mid-2017 → Oct-2018):** undated — no archived store snapshot in 2018 and no indexed FB posts that far back.
- **Point releases** between the milestones above (e.g. 2.6.5–2.6.9, 3.1.0–3.1.2, 3.2.0) are only bracketed by their neighbours.
- **Exact intro versions** for PvP Arena, Guild War, pets, and the Honor-Unit system itself — they predate the oldest recoverable notes (present by launch / 1.x).
- The original iOS listing (`id1073014391`, 2016) is delisted with **zero** Wayback snapshots; the current listing (`id1339001756`) was re-published Feb 2018, so its metadata wrongly implies a 2018 launch.
- **No Korean patch archive exists** — despite heavy Korean searching (namu.wiki/Naver/Inven), this title has no Korean wiki/forum coverage; Ekkorr ran patch comms in **English** on Facebook.
